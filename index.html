Why is it so difficult to source Passive Candidates?
All present recruitment systems & methods are completely focused towards active job seekers. Passive Prospects experience a apparent disconnect when approached through these channels. Below are some key reasons why the conventional tools of recruitemnet dont work of these ambitious successful and highly desirable group of candidates.
  • Passive Prospects are already busy and successful in their career. Hence they dont feel a need to have an updated Resume or a current LinkedIn Profile
  • They get frequenty unsolicited head hunter calls and so they will never reply back!
  • They are not yet at a stage where they would want to move organizations. So they seldom read your job postings
At Engage our research has led us to a depper understanding of the ambitious career people. Passive candidates are always interested in what the latest trends of their industry are and how they can improve their skillset to advance. They are curious about how other companies work and what's their culture like.
